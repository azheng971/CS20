What are we about? ✨




Life in Chapters provides weekly prompts for you to have great conversations with the people you care about, and turns those meaningful moments into a beautiful memoir. We can also help you write your own memoir for future generations to read. Our mission is to preserve and share humanity’s stories in order to build connections between people, and discover stories you never knew.



✨How does it work? ✨

Step 01: Weekly Prompts

Once a week, choose a question to inspire someone to write.


Step 02: Record

You may record what they said in the form of an in-person or virtual conversation, or you can send them the prompt via our email link and their written response will be shared with you.


Step 03: Get Your Book

At the end of the year, their stories are bound into a beautiful keepsake book with writings and photos of your choice.

Life in Chapters provide books designed to last for generations to come. We’ve sourced quality materials that look great now and age gracefully over time to honor the stories within. If life is a book, what would this chapter be?
